Attendance Rules & Integrity Check (MANDATORY)

  • 1. Clear Visibility & Environment: Ensure your entire face is clearly visible within the frame. Avoid strong backlighting, shadows, or low-light conditions.
  • 2. No Face Obstructions: Remove any items like masks, glasses (if they obstruct critical facial features), or hats during the verification process. Your full facial structure must be visible.
  • 3. Mandatory Liveness Check: You must immediately follow the on-screen instructions (e.g., blink, turn your head, nod) to complete the liveness detection and prove you are a live person.
  • 4. Zero Tolerance for Spoofing: The use of photos, videos, 3D models, or any form of digital manipulation to mark attendance is **strictly prohibited**. The system uses advanced AI anti-spoofing technology.
  • 5. Critical Disciplinary Charges: Any confirmed attempt to spoof or fraudulently mark attendance will result in **immediate and severe disciplinary action**, including a permanent failing grade for the course and critical misconduct charges.
